At trulinenetwork.us, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our internet, cable, and phone services. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information from you, including:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, postal address, email address, phone number, billing information, account credentials (username and password), and other information that identifies you or can be used to identify you.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our website and services, including your IP address, browser type, device information (operating system, hardware model), referring website, pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, and other usage data. This may also include information about your use of our internet, cable, and phone services, such as call logs, data usage, and service performance.
  • Location Information: With your permission, we may collect information about your device's location. This information may be used to provide you with location-based services and offers. You can control the collection of your location information through your device settings.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, analyze website traffic, and track user activity. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver and manage our internet, cable, and phone services, including account management, billing, customer support, service provisioning, and troubleshooting.
  • Improving Services: To analyze usage trends, personalize your experience, develop new services and features, conduct research and analysis, and improve the performance and functionality of our website and services.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional materials, newsletters, offers, and updates about our services and those of our partners, with the option to opt out at any time. We will only send marketing communications if you have opted in to receive them, or if we are otherwise permitted to do so under applicable law.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and government requests.
  • Security: To protect the security and integrity of our website, services, and customer data, including detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors and partners who assist us in delivering our services, such as billing and payment processing, customer support, technical support, marketing, and analytics. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to legal authorities if required by law, court order, government request, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: If trulinenetwork.us is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Affiliates: We may share your information with our affiliated companies for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include physical, technical, and administrative safeguards. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. We encourage you to use strong passwords and take other precautions to protect your account and personal information.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-out: You have the right to opt out of marketing communications at any time. You can do this by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in our marketing emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Cookie Control: You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
